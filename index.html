Fundamentals

  • Roth IRA vs Individual Account: A Roth IRA offers tax-free growth and withdrawals in retirement but has contribution and income limits. An individual brokerage account has no limits, but dividends and capital gains are taxable.
  • Roth IRA vs 401k: Roth IRAs are funded with after-tax dollars and grow tax-free. 401ks are usually pre-tax and taxed at withdrawal, often with employer matching.
  • What is an ETF: An ETF is a basket of investments that trades like a stock, offering diversification and usually low fees.
  • What are dividends: Dividends are profit payments companies distribute to shareholders, which can be reinvested or taken as income.
  • Why invest: Investing helps grow wealth over time, beat inflation, and take advantage of compound interest.
