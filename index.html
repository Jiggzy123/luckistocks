Fundamentals

  • Roth IRA vs Individual Account: Roth IRAs grow tax-free but have limits. Individual accounts have no limits but are taxable.
  • Roth IRA vs 401k: Roth IRAs are after-tax. 401ks are pre-tax and often include employer matching.
  • What is an ETF: A basket of assets that trades like a stock.
  • What are dividends: Profit payments to shareholders.
  • Why invest: To beat inflation and compound wealth.

Stocks Explained

A stock represents ownership in a company. Stocks grow through price appreciation and dividends. They are more volatile than ETFs but offer higher growth potential over long periods.
