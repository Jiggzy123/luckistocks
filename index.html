Fundamentals

  • Roth IRA vs Individual Account: Roth IRAs grow tax-free but have limits. Individual accounts have no limits but are taxable.
  • Roth IRA vs 401k: Roth IRAs are after-tax. 401ks are usually pre-tax and may include employer match.
  • What is an ETF: ETFs bundle many stocks into one investment that trades like a stock.
  • What are dividends: Cash payments from company profits paid to shareholders.
  • Why invest: Investing beats inflation and compounds wealth over time.

Stocks Explained

A stock represents ownership in a company. When you buy stock, you own a piece of that business and benefit from growth, profits, and sometimes dividends.
