Individual Brokerage Account vs Roth IRA

Individual Brokerage Account

An individual brokerage account offers flexibility and no contribution limits. However, dividends and capital gains are taxable.

Roth IRA

A Roth IRA grows tax-free. Contributions are taxed upfront, but qualified withdrawals in retirement are completely tax-free.

Taxes & Compound Interest

Avoiding taxes allows compound interest to accelerate growth over long periods.
