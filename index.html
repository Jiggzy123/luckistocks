Fundamentals

  • Roth IRA vs Individual Account: A Roth IRA grows tax-free but has contribution and income limits. An individual brokerage account has no limits but is taxable.
  • Roth IRA vs 401k: Roth IRAs use after-tax dollars; 401ks are usually pre-tax and may include employer matching.
  • What is an ETF: An ETF is a collection of stocks that trades like a stock and provides instant diversification.
  • What are dividends: Dividends are cash payments companies give shareholders from profits.
  • What are stocks: Stocks represent ownership in a company. As the company grows, the value of your shares can increase.
  • Why invest: Investing helps grow wealth, beat inflation, and compound money over time.
