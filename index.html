Fundamentals

  • Roth IRA vs Individual Account: A Roth IRA allows tax-free growth and withdrawals in retirement but has limits. Individual accounts have no limits but are taxable.
  • Roth IRA vs 401k: Roth IRAs grow tax-free. 401ks are pre-tax and often include employer matching.
  • What is an ETF: A bundle of stocks in one fund that trades like a stock.
  • What are dividends: Payments companies give shareholders from profits.
  • Why invest: To beat inflation and compound wealth over time.

Stocks Explained

Stocks represent ownership in a company. They grow through appreciation and dividends, but are more volatile than ETFs.
