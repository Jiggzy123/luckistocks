Fundamentals

  • Roth IRA vs Individual Account: Roth IRAs grow tax-free but have limits. Individual accounts have no limits but are taxable.
  • Roth IRA vs 401k: Roth = tax-free later. 401k = pre-tax now + employer match.
  • ETF: A basket of investments traded like a stock.
  • Dividends: Cash paid to shareholders from profits.
  • Why invest: Beat inflation and build long-term wealth.
