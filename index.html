Fundamentals

  • Roth IRA vs Individual Account: A Roth IRA grows tax-free and allows tax-free withdrawals in retirement, but has income and contribution limits. An individual brokerage account has no limits but is taxed on dividends and gains.
  • Roth IRA vs 401k: A Roth IRA is after-tax and tax-free later. A 401k is usually pre-tax and taxed on withdrawal, often with employer matching.
  • What is an ETF: An ETF is a basket of investments that trades like a stock and provides instant diversification.
  • What are dividends: Dividends are payments companies give shareholders from profits, paid in cash or reinvested.
  • Why invest: Investing grows money over time, beats inflation, and builds long-term wealth through compounding.
