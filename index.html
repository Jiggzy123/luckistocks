Recommended ETFs

VOO

Tracks the S&P 500, providing exposure to the 500 largest U.S. companies and serving as a strong long-term core holding.

VXUS

Provides international diversification by investing in companies outside of the United States.

SCHD

Focuses on dividend-paying U.S. companies, offering income and stability alongside growth.

TECH ETFs

QQQM

Tracks the Nasdaq-100, offering exposure to large-cap growth and technology-focused companies.

FTEC

A low-expense ratio technology ETF covering a broad range of U.S. tech companies.

VGT

Provides concentrated exposure to the information technology sector with long-term growth potential.

Note: I recommend choosing only one tech ETF. Multiple options are shown to compare strategies, not to hold all simultaneously.
