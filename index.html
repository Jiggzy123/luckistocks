Recommended ETFs

VOO

VOO tracks the S&P 500, offering broad exposure to large U.S. companies with extremely low fees.

Technology ETFs

Choose one to avoid overlap:

  • QQQM – Tracks the NASDAQ-100, focusing on large-cap growth and tech-heavy companies.
  • FTEC – Broad U.S. technology exposure with a very low expense ratio.
  • VGT – Vanguard’s diversified U.S. technology ETF.

SCHD

Dividend-focused ETF emphasizing stability and consistent income.

VXUS

Provides exposure to international markets outside the U.S.
