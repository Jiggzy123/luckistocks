Recommended ETFs

VOO

Vanguard S&P 500 ETF. Tracks the S&P 500 and gives exposure to the 500 largest U.S. companies. Very low cost and a solid long-term foundation.

QQQM / FTEC / VGT

Tech-focused ETFs. Higher growth potential but more volatile. These are long-term holds and should not be traded emotionally.

SCHD

Dividend-focused ETF. More stable and provides income over time.

VXUS

International exposure outside the U.S. Helps with diversification.
