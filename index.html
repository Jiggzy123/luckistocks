Recommended ETFs

VOO

Tracks the S&P 500, offering broad exposure to large U.S. companies. A strong long-term core holding.

QQQM / FTEC / VGT

Technology-focused ETFs providing exposure to major growth companies like Apple, Microsoft, and NVIDIA.

SCHD

Dividend-focused ETF emphasizing high-quality U.S. companies with consistent payouts.

VXUS

Provides international diversification by investing in non-U.S. developed and emerging markets.
